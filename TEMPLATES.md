# templates

there's currently a selection of 12 templates available. 10 are from limbs of
the unknown (lotu), 1 is the default template, and 1 is a template for surf
levels.

all of the lotu templates are quite basic, but significant.

 - default
  - no changes
 - lotu: air control
  - mass: 1 => 0.0001
  - jumpForce: 6 => 10
 - lotu: bouncy ball
  - bounce: 0.25 => 0.9

  - coyoteTime: 0.3 => 2
 - lotu: heavy ball
  - mass: 1 => 2
 - lotu: high fall tolerance
  - damageStartImpac: 17.3 => 40
 - lotu: low gravity
  - gravityScale: 1 => 0.5
 - lotu: short ball
  - cameraHeight: 0.9 => 3.281e-304
 - lotu: tall ball
